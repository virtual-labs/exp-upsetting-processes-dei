<h2>Variables in Upset Forging Process</h2>
<hr>

<h3>EFFECT OF FORGING EQUIPMENT ON FORGING PROCESS</h3>
<p>
Each forging press or machine (mechanical, hydraulic, screw, hammer) has unique stroke characteristics.<br>
One important variable that affects the forming process is press ram speed.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Hydraulic Press → constant ram speed</li>
<li>Mechanical Press → variable ram speed</li>
</ul>
